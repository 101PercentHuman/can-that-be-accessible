## Welcome

When things are accessible, you don't notice them at all. 

And that means people with disability will notice a lot. There are barriers all around us, and we can always do more to get rid of these barriers—and make accessibility normal.

When you plan properly, you plan for accessibility.

Let's see who did it right and who didn't, and see what we can learn.
